testing notes:- Smart Water Purification System — Testing Notes

I tested the system multiple times to understand whether the TDS sensor readings were reliable and whether the water purification setup could work consistently.

The system was able to measure water quality and display readings on the LCD screen. The pump also worked to move water through the filtration setup when required.

At the same time, there were a lot of small issues during testing.

Sometimes the TDS readings fluctuated more than expected, especially after repeated testing. Small setup changes, water consistency, and sensor placement sometimes affected the readings.

Managing smooth water flow through the filtration setup also took trial and error. Some tests worked properly, while others required recalibration and adjustments.

One thing I realized quickly was that building a working prototype is very different from building something reliable enough for real-world use. Small hardware issues become much bigger when consistency matters.

Even though it was not perfect, the project taught me a lot about sensors, calibration, debugging, and practical problem solving.

Things I would improve:

* Better TDS calibration
* More reliable filtration system
* Improved water flow consistency
* Real-time monitoring dashboard
* IoT-based monitoring
